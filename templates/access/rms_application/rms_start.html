Revenue Management User Security Agreement

This form is to request access to the Revenue Management System

Instructions

Section I: General User Information

A. In order to allow access to the Revenue Management System the form must be completed
B. Provide full information and sign. Mobile phone will be included in the Revenue Management System as part of your contact information.
C. Email address is required. Please provide an official email address.



Section II: Access and Group Selection

A. Select user access by choosing one of the levels of permission
B. Select the Division/Department/Park/Station which you are deployed
C. Forms with invalid permission request will be rejected.



Section III: User Account Request

A. Print your name and date acknowledging that you have read, understand RMS Security Agreement and will comply with RMS security requirements. Enter your email address in the Signature block to sign the form electronically.
B. Attach the document and send via e-mail or forward to your Department Head Requestor for approval.
